Exception Kindc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/wipo-ip#ExceptionKind

the same Copyright Ontology (Garcia & Gil, JURIX 2008) lists seven exceptions: Quotation, Education, Information, Official Act, Private Copy, Parody, Temporary Reproduction. PrivateUseException and ParodyException match exactly (Private Copy, Parody); ResearchException, CriticismReviewException and NewsReportingException are only approximate matches to Education, Quotation and Information respectively, not identical in scope, flagged here rather than assumed equivalent.

Right Kindc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/wipo-ip#RightKind

matches the Economic Rights hierarchy in Garcia & Gil's Copyright Ontology (JURIX 2008): Reproduction, Distribution, Communication (with Broadcasting and Making Available as subtypes), Transformation (with Adaptation and Translation as subtypes), Fixation. That hierarchy refines the four top-level ExploitationRight classes (Reproduction, Communication, Distribution, Transformation) in Delgado et al.'s earlier IPROnto (JURIX 2003). All eight individuals below have a direct counterpart in the 2008 version.
is in range of
includes act op
has members
Adaptation Act ni, Broadcasting Act ni, Communication To Public Act ni, Distribution Act ni, Making Available Act ni, Recording Act ni, Reproduction Act ni, Translation Act ni

containsop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/wipo-ip#contains

range is lrmlmm:RuleStatement, LegalRuleML's own formal common superclass of ConstitutiveStatement and PrescriptiveStatement (see statement.rdf in the OASIS metamodel RDFS modules), not an ad-hoc class of ours. Used for both constitutive statements (definitions, eligibility rules) and prescriptive statements (rights, exceptions). Fixed 2026-07-22: an earlier draft wrongly restricted this to ConstitutiveStatement only; fixed 2026-07-23: gave it a real range instead of leaving it open, which had made it resolve to owl:Thing in WebVOWL.

includes actop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/wipo-ip#includesAct

CQ2. Which acts a copyright covers; a country can list several. Mapping these across countries is an approximation, not a formal equivalence: Ireland's single 'make available to the public' is treated here as the closest match to what India and Kenya sometimes split into distribution, communication to the public, and making available as separate items. Flagged here, not silently assumed as equal.
